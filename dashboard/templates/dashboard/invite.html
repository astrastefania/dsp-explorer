Help us to achieve makers, innovators and thinkers in the current 4th Industrial revolution!

If you know someone with great ideas, projects and visions around new forms of production and making, give us their contact and we will invite them to join our movement, participate in our events, and get in touch with a broader community of innovators.

Be aware that OpenMaker manages personal contacts in a responsible way: contacts are used only to send an invitation e-mail to the nominated person, and our systems will ensure that multiple emails are avoided.
The contacts that you provide will not be exploited for commercial purposes, and will not be passed to third parties who may do so.

If you wish to get more information about the OpenMaker’s chain of nomination, contact us!
