Some of our Members

Roberto Rubini

He is graduated in Urban Planning and Architecture. He is a founder of Source, Self Made Design, which was born five years ago as a Self-Product Design Exhibition and today has become a company.
The goal is to connect the world of creatives and designers with production and distribution companies through workshops, trainings and networking events.

Giacomo Falaschi

He is Vice-President of Fablab Toscana, founder of Falla3d and co-fouder of FabLab Contea.
He oversees the opening of an internal FabLab in Ferrovie dello Stato, the National Rails company. He is an acknowledged trainer of the Fab Academy.

Daniele D'Arrigo

He is a maker who developed several open source aids solutions for disabled people. He is collaborating with public assistance institutions with several experimental programs
He is now working with Paleos in the development of Fabsussidiario, a toolkit for the learning by making in schools.
